About Maison Café

Specialty Coffee, Thoughtfully Served

At Maison Café, we source premium beans and focus on precise brewing methods to highlight every note and aroma. Our baristas blend craftsmanship with hospitality to create an elevated coffee ritual.

Brunch Culture, Reimagined

Inspired by Dubai’s vibrant brunch scene, our kitchen serves fresh, balanced plates made to pair beautifully with your coffee. From comforting classics to wholesome bowls, Maison Café is designed for slow mornings and stylish gatherings.
